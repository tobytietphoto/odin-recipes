Vietnamese Pho recipe

This recipe is made from scratch with a signature broth that is light yet full of flavor at the same time.

Ingredients

  • 3 lb brisket
  • 2 lb meaty bones
  • 2 lb marrow bones
  • ginger
  • onion
  • cinnamon
  • cardamom
  • coriander
  • star anise
  • sugar
  • salt
  • fish sauce

Steps

  1. Quick boil to remove impurities from beef with a 5 minute boil
  2. Be amazed at all the icky stuff that comes out
  3. Wash the bones to get all the scum off
  4. Put bones, beef, water, onion, ginger and spices (cinnamon, cardamom, coriander, star anise) into pot and simmer for 3 hours
  5. Remove brisket to be used for topping
  6. Simmer for another 40 minutes with just bones
  7. Strain soup
  8. Ladle the soup into bowls of noodles and toppings
