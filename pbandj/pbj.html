Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

The ultimate comfort meal! Its easy to make and delicious. If you dont agree, then you are wrong and

I hate you.

Ingredients

  • get bread of choice into slices
  • tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1 or 2 teaspoons of jam of choice

Recipe

  1. Spread the peanut butter onto one piece of bread
  2. Spread the jelly onto the other piece of bread
  3. Combine both piece of bread together to form sandwich
  4. Enjoy!
